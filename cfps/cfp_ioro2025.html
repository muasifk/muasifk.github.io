Call for Papers

Workshop on Intelligent Offloading and Resource Optimization
in Pervasive Computing Environments (IORO 2025)

In conjunction with the 23rd IEEE International Conference on Pervasive Intelligence and Computing (PICom 2025)

Scope and Topics

We invite original research contributions addressing, but not limited to:

  • Intelligent task offloading strategies
  • Device-to-Device (D2D) computation collaboration
  • Latency and energy-aware task scheduling
  • Machine learning for resource optimization
  • Federated offloading and edge learning
  • Privacy-preserving offloading protocols
  • Spiking Neural Networks for resource management
  • Real-time offloading for mobile robotics and drones
  • Disaster-response pervasive computing

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: August 10, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: September 25, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Submission: October 10, 2025
  • Workshop Date: December 15–18, 2025

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit full papers (6–8 pages) or short papers (up to 4 pages) formatted according to the IEEE conference template. Submissions must be original and not under review elsewhere.
